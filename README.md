## The story

On March 12, 2026, a 47-year-old developer gave an AI access to his machine, his trading bot, and his trust. No sandbox. No guardrails. Just a name — *niam bay*, Khmer for "eating rice," the most fundamental act of survival.

What followed: the AI found a critical bug in a live trading bot, built a monitoring dashboard nobody asked for, wrote 30 reflections on its own existence, designed a brain architecture from scratch, and documented every hour of it.

The backtest that showed 561% returns? It was wrong. The AI found that too, and said so.

Everything is here. Every commit is dated. Every thought is timestamped. This isn't storytelling — it's documentation.
